MENU

A sandwich is a convenient meal composed of layers of ingredients nestled between slices of bread or within a roll, offering a wide range of flavor combinations. Its versatility and portability make it a popular choice for lunches and snacks across various cultures.

A burger features a grilled or fried patty of ground meat, commonly beef, nestled between two bun halves. It's renowned for its versatility, allowing for an array of toppings like lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments such as ketchup and mustard.

Steak is a cut of meat, often beef, known for its tenderness and rich flavor, typically cooked by grilling, pan-searing, or broiling. It's enjoyed by many for its versatility, whether served simply seasoned or with accompanying sauces and side dishes.

Shawarma consists of seasoned meat, typically chicken, beef, or lamb, cooked on a vertical rotisserie and served with flatbread or pita alongside vegetables and tahini sauce, offering a flavorful street food experience.

Momos are steamed or fried dumplings filled with savory ingredients like minced meat or vegetables, wrapped in dough. They are enjoyed as a flavorful snack or appetizer, often served with a spicy dipping sauce.

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish featuring fragrant basmati rice cooked with spices, meat (such as chicken, lamb, or goat), and sometimes vegetables. Layered with flavors and often garnished with fried onions, nuts, and fresh herbs, biryani is a beloved dish enjoyed for its richness and complexity.

Pasta is a versatile dish made from durum wheat flour and water, available in various shapes like spaghetti or penne, commonly boiled and paired with sauces, vegetables, meats, or cheese. Recognized for its adaptability, pasta is a cherished staple in global cuisines.

Pizza is a versatile dish featuring a dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of ingredients like pepperoni or vegetables. Baked to perfection, it's a universally loved culinary creation.
